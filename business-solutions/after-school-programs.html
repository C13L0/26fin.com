After School Programs

Parents rely on reputable after school programs to provide a safe, fun environment for their children, make managing the process as easy on them as possible

Offer parents the ability to pay for your program online or in-person with their credit or debit card and you'll see a stronger revenue stream and better planning.

When parents are picking up their kids from after school, they need simplicity and flexibility in their options. Offer them the ability to choose how to pay for your after school program online with a credit or debit card when it is convenient for them - with recurring credit or ECH payments, or in person when they are in your facility.
