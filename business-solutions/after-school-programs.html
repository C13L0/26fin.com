After School Programs

Parents rely on reputable after school programs to provide a safe, fun environment for their children, make managing the process as easy on them as possible

Offer the parents of your students the ability to pay for your program online or in-person with their credit or debit card and you'll see a stronger revenue stream and better planning.

When parents are picking up their kids from after school, they need simplicity and flexibility in their options. Offer them the ability to choose to pay their after school program online with a credit or debit card when it is convenient for them, by handing you a credit card when they come pick up their children, or accept their check at the specified times.
