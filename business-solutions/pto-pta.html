Parent Teacher Organization / Parent Teacher Association

Increase your group's income and ability to grant money to you school with increase revenue collection

Parent-run, school-based organizations have the ability to raise a generous amount of funds from donations, fundraisers and programs - and by using TwoSix Financial's solutions, that revenue can be realized, collected and tracked with ease.

Credit and Debit Card Acceptance

Accept credit and debit cards for spirit wear, book fairs, school supply purchases, and other fundraising programs

Check Acceptance

Parents are still asked to pay for several items throughout the school year and most tend to send a check in with their child rather than cash or a credit card number. TwoSix Financial can accept and validate these checks immediately and your organization can realize this revenue faster.

Loyalty / Gift Cards

Customize a gift card for spirit wear or other school related merchandise and use as an incentive for students and parents alike. This keeps your money within your organization and offers a unique branding option.
