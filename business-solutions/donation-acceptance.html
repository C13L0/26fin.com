Donation Acceptance

Fundraising is the lifeline of many organizations - increase your donations by offering convenient payment options to potential donors

Whether your organization runs solely on the generosity of others, or you use donations as a supplement to further your mission, the more options and convenience you offer your donors, the more money you can collect.

  • Accept credit and debit cards at auctions, book fairs, and other fundraising events - and realize immediate payment collections
  • Provide donors easy options to make donation
  • Utilize accurate accounting of monies collected
